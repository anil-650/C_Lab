## 3. Practice use of image, video and sound in HTML documents.

```html
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
<meta charset="UTF-8">
<title>Document</title>
</head>
<body>
<h3>Music</h3>
<audio controls>
<source src="play.ogg" type="audio/ogg">
<source src="play.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
</audio><br>
<h3>Video</h3>
<video width="250px" height="250px" controls>
<source src="video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
<source src="video.ogg" type="audio/ogg">
</video><br>
<h3>Image</h3>
<img src="image.jpg" height="150px" width="150px">
</body>
</html>
```
